About

My Story

My name is Natalie Calhoun and I graduated from Royal Holloway, University of London with a bachelor’s degree in Management with Marketing. I spent two years doing marketing at a startup software company, where I did everything from social media marketing to maintaining the look of the website to designing all print and digital marketing materials.

I also have owned a painting business for the past seven years, creating miniature paintings and selling them on my Etsy store. Both of these experiences have helped me realize my love for design and the importance of a seamless user experience. When I’m not designing, I love to explore other creative outlets such as painting, gardening, and crocheting.
